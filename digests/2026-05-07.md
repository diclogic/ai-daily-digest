## Notable Papers & Research

**Terminus-4B: Can a Smaller Model Replace Frontier LLMs at Agentic Execution Tasks?**
Explores whether a 4-billion-parameter model can match frontier LLMs on agentic coding and tool-use tasks, contributing to the growing "small models, big performance" research direction.
[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/current)

**MEMTIER: Tiered Memory Architecture and Retrieval Bottleneck Analysis for Long-Running Autonomous AI Agents**
Proposes a tiered memory system for autonomous agents running over extended time horizons, identifying retrieval as the key bottleneck rather than storage capacity.
[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/current)

**Agentic AI Orchestration Should be Bayes-consistent** (Position Paper)
Argues that the control layer of agentic AI systems must be grounded in Bayesian principles to ensure reliable decision-making under uncertainty. Submitted May 4.
[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/current)

**Stop Automating Peer Review Without Rigorous Evaluation** — ICML 2026 Spotlight
Position paper calling for systematic evaluation standards before deploying AI in academic peer review, citing risks of feedback homogenization and bias amplification.
[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/current)

### Anthropic–SpaceX Colossus Compute Deal — May 6
Anthropic signed a deal to use all compute capacity at SpaceX/xAI's Colossus 1 data center in Memphis — over 300 MW and 220,000+ NVIDIA GPUs (H100, H200, GB200). The deal directly addresses capacity constraints from explosive demand. Anthropic also "expressed interest" in developing multi-gigawatt compute capacity in space with SpaceX.
[CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/06/anthropic-spacex-data-center-capacity.html) | [xAI](https://x.ai/news/anthropic-compute-partnership) | [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-05-06/anthropic-inks-computing-deal-with-spacex-to-meet-ai-demand)

## Key Opinions & Statements

### Dario Amodei (Anthropic CEO)
**On 80x growth:** "I hope that 80-times growth doesn't continue because that's just crazy and it's too hard to handle." Anthropic's revenue run rate surpassed $30B, up from $9B at end of 2025, driven largely by Claude Code adoption. The company had planned for 10x growth but was blindsided by demand.
[CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/06/anthropic-ceo-dario-amodei-says-company-crew-80-fold-in-first-quarter.html)

**On SaaS disruption:** Warned that software companies whose moat is "our software is complex and difficult to write" will see that advantage erode. "Individual SaaS companies, it's very possible for them to lose market value, go bankrupt, completely go bust."
[Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/anthropic-ceo-dario-amodei-warns-some-software-companies-will-completely-go-bust-191014499.html)

**On China competition:** Warned US firms have a 6-12 month lead before Chinese AI models develop comparable vulnerability-discovery capabilities, urging immediate action to patch critical software.
[India News Network](https://www.indianewsnetwork.com/en/anthropic-ceo-dario-amodei-warns-us-firms-ai-competition-china-20260507)

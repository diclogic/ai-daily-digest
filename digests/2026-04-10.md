## Model & Product Launches

### Claude Mythos Preview — Anthropic ⚠️ [SPECIAL WATCHLIST]
*Announced April 7, 2026*

Anthropic's most powerful model to date — codenamed Capybara, existence first leaked via a March 2026 content management error — has been given a restricted "Preview" release. The model achieved **93.9% on SWE-bench Verified** and **97.6% on USAMO 2026**, and can autonomously discover and chain zero-day exploits across all major operating systems and browsers.

Rather than a public release, Anthropic launched **Project Glasswing**: 50+ founding partner organizations — including Amazon Web Services, Apple, Broadcom, Cisco, CrowdStrike, Google, JPMorganChase, Microsoft, NVIDIA, and Palo Alto Networks — receive gated access with over $100M in usage credits for defensive cybersecurity work. In testing, the model found thousands of high-severity vulnerabilities, including a 27-year-old flaw in OpenBSD and multiple Linux kernel vulnerabilities that could grant complete machine control.

Sources: [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/07/anthropic-mythos-ai-model-preview-security/) · [NBC News](https://www.nbcnews.com/tech/security/anthropic-project-glasswing-mythos-preview-claude-gets-limited-release-rcna267234) · [NxCode breakdown](https://www.nxcode.io/resources/news/claude-mythos-preview-anthropic-most-powerful-model-2026) · [CrowdStrike](https://www.crowdstrike.com/en-us/blog/crowdstrike-founding-member-anthropic-mythos-frontier-model-to-secure-ai/)

### Meta Muse Spark
*Announced April 8, 2026*

Meta's first major model release under Meta Superintelligence Labs (led by Alexandr Wang, hired via a $14B deal) and the first of the new "Muse" model family. Muse Spark accepts voice, text, and image inputs (text output only) and ships with three interaction modes: Instant, Thinking, and the upcoming Contemplating. Its biggest differentiation is health: **42.8 on HealthBench Hard** — nearly triple Gemini (20.6) and Claude Opus 4.6 (14.8). Ranked 4th on the Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index v4.0 (score: 52). Currently US-only; will power Meta AI, smart glasses, and all Meta social apps.

Sources: [Meta AI Blog](https://ai.meta.com/blog/introducing-muse-spark-msl) · [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/08/meta-debuts-the-muse-spark-model-in-a-ground-up-overhaul-of-its-ai/) · [Simon Willison](https://simonwillison.net/2026/Apr/8/muse-spark/)

**Andrej Karpathy** shared an "LLM Second Brain" architecture (April 3) that has gone viral among developers. Rather than using AI to write code, Karpathy now uses it to build and maintain an auto-updating, interlinked markdown wiki from raw research inputs — no RAG required. His own research wiki on a single topic grew to ~100 articles and 400,000 words. He also demonstrated an OpenClaw agent called "Dobby" (April 1) that autonomously scanned his home network, reverse-engineered undocumented device APIs, and gained control of Sonos and lighting systems — a pointed demonstration of how capable local agents have become.

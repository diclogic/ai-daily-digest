## Key Opinions & Statements

### Dario Amodei — White House Meeting on Mythos Access
Anthropic CEO met Friday with White House Chief of Staff Susie Wiles and Treasury Secretary Scott Bessent. The US government is seeking wider access to Claude Mythos for cybersecurity defense, despite having blacklisted Anthropic after the Pentagon dispute. Amodei has refused to allow unrestricted military use of Anthropic's AI — insisting on restrictions against mass surveillance and autonomous weapons. An Anthropic spokesperson called it "a productive discussion." The meeting is significant because the Pentagon previously designated Anthropic a "supply chain risk" — a label typically reserved for foreign adversaries.

## Model & Product Launches

### GPT-5.5 Instant (OpenAI) — Released May 5
OpenAI's new default ChatGPT model replaces GPT-5.3 Instant. Key improvements include 52.5% fewer hallucinated claims on high-stakes prompts, ~30% more concise responses, and deeper personalization from past conversations and connected services. Available in API as `chat-latest`. ([OpenAI Blog](https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-5-instant/) | [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/05/openai-releases-gpt-5-5-instant-a-new-default-model-for-chatgpt/))

### SubQ 1M-Preview (Subquadratic) — Released May 5
Miami startup Subquadratic emerged from stealth with $29M in seed funding and the first frontier LLM built on a fully subquadratic architecture. Their Selective Sparse Attention (SSA) mechanism scales linearly with context length, enabling a 12M-token context window at ~1/5th the cost of transformers. Benchmarks: 95.0% RULER 128K, 81.8% SWE-Bench Verified. Researchers demand independent verification of the 1,000x efficiency claims. ([SubQ](https://subq.ai/introducing-subq) | [VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/technology/miami-startup-subquadratic-claims-1-000x-ai-efficiency-gain-with-subq-model-researchers-demand-independent-proof))

### Gemini 3.1 Flash Lite (Google) — Released May 8
New lightweight Gemini variant optimized for gateway and edge use cases, now available through Google AI Studio and Vertex AI.

### Claude Mythos Preview (Anthropic) — Update
Remains in limited access through Project Glasswing. The model has now identified thousands of zero-day vulnerabilities across every major OS and browser, including a 27-year-old OpenBSD flaw and a 16-year-old FFmpeg vulnerability. Reproduces exploits on first attempt in 83% of cases. Access limited to ~40 vetted critical-infrastructure organizations. Pricing: $25/$125 per million input/output tokens. ([Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/glasswing) | [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/05/anthropic-ceo-cyber-moment-of-danger-mythos-vulnerabilities.html))

Supports text-to-video, image generation, and chat-based editing with character/scene consistency across shots. Also leaked: Gemini 3.2 Flash (sub-200ms responses) and a new Gemini model expected to rival GPT-5.5. ([Android Authority](https://www.androidauthority.com/what-to-expect-from-google-io-2026-3664979/) | [AIxploria](https://www.aixploria.com/en/ai-radar/google-gemini-omni-leak-video-model-io-2026/))

## Key Opinions & Statements

### Dario Amodei (Anthropic CEO)
In a conversation with Jamie Dimon at JPMorgan's financial services briefing, Amodei invoked the **Jevons Paradox** to argue that AI-driven productivity gains will create *more* demand for knowledge workers rather than eliminating jobs — a notable shift from his earlier warnings about an AI "white-collar bloodbath." Separately, he warned that Mythos has created a "moment of danger" for cybersecurity, where AI-discovered vulnerabilities must be patched before malicious actors can exploit similar capabilities. ([Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/05/05/dario-amodei-jevons-paradox-will-ai-wipe-out-white-collar-jobs/) | [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/05/anthropic-ceo-cyber-moment-of-danger-mythos-vulnerabilities.html))

### Yann LeCun (AMI Labs, formerly Meta AI)
Called CEO warnings about AI job loss "extremely destructive," arguing they cause students to abandon technical careers based on exaggerated claims. LeCun noted that historically it takes 15 years for new technologies to achieve promised productivity gains. His startup AMI ($1B raised) continues building world-model AI systems that move beyond next-token prediction. ([Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/05/05/ai-job-apocalypse-warnings-destructive-yann-lecun/) | [Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/05/04/ai-godfather-survival-guide-hype-doom))

### Andrej Karpathy
At Sequoia's AI Ascent 2026, Karpathy outlined the shift from "vibe coding" to "agentic engineering" — arguing developers are becoming editors, reviewers, and system designers rather than line-by-line coders. He claims he hasn't personally written a line of code since December 2025, relying entirely on AI agents. ([Sequoia AI Ascent](https://podcasts.apple.com/ec/podcast/andrej-karpathy-at-sequoia-ai-ascent-2026-from-vibe/id1799918505?i=1000765806763&l=en-GB) | [Frank's World](https://www.franksworld.com/2026/05/01/andrej-karpathy-on-the-evolution-from-vibe-coding-to-agentic-engineering/))

### Demis Hassabis (Google DeepMind CEO)
During a Korea visit marking AlphaGo's 10th anniversary, Hassabis stated that AGI is "likely to arrive in 3-4 years" and could bring "profound change to industries and society." Announced expanded DeepMind partnership with Korea's Ministry of Science for AI safety research and student talent development. ([EdTech Innovation Hub](https://www.edtechinnovationhub.com/news/demis-hassabis-marks-alphago-anniversary-with-renewed-google-deepmind-push-in-korea))

## Other Notable AI News

### Musk v. Altman Trial Heads to Jury
The landmark trial concluded closing arguments May 14. Jury deliberations begin May 19. The nine-person advisory jury will weigh whether Altman and Brockman violated charitable-trust law when converting OpenAI to an $852B for-profit. Musk alleges his $38M donation was diverted; Altman counters that Musk "abandoned" the charity after seeking 90% equity. Judge Gonzalez Rogers makes the final ruling. ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/14/closing-arguments-jury-openai-musk-altman.html) | [TechTimes](https://www.techtimes.com/articles/316720/20260516/openai-jury-weighs-removal-altman-134b-penalty-deliberations-open-oakland.htm))

### Anthropic's 80x Growth Forces SpaceX Data Center Deal
Anthropic reported 80-fold annualized revenue growth in Q1 2026, reaching a $30B run rate. To handle demand, the company is renting Colossus 1 — Elon Musk's xAI data center in Memphis — gaining access to 220,000 NVIDIA GPUs and 300MW of power. Additional capacity: 5 GW through AWS (1 GW by year-end) and 5 GW with Google/Broadcom (2027). ([Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/05/08/anthropic-80fold-growth-quarter-renting-elon-musk-data-center/) | [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/06/anthropic-ceo-dario-amodei-says-company-crew-80-fold-in-first-quarter.html))

### OpenAI Creates Deployment Company
OpenAI announced a new subsidiary, OpenAI Deployment Company, backed by $4B+ in initial investment. The unit embeds engineering teams within enterprises to accelerate AI adoption, and acquired AI consultancy Tomoro (~150 engineers). ([OpenAI News](https://openai.com/news/))

### Novo Nordisk × OpenAI Strategic Partnership
Danish pharma giant partnered with OpenAI to integrate AI across drug discovery, clinical trials, and supply chains, aiming to accelerate development of obesity and diabetes treatments.

### Meta Llama 4 Launch
Meta released Llama 4, its most capable open-source model, designed to compete with GPT-4.5 and Gemini Ultra. Features improved reasoning, multilingual support, and efficient inference for edge deployment.

## Model & Product Launches

### Alibaba Qwen 3.6-Max-Preview (April 20)
Alibaba released its most capable model yet — a 35B-parameter MoE (3B active) that claims #1 on six coding and agent benchmarks including SWE-bench Pro, Terminal-Bench 2.0, and SciCode. It supports a 256K context window and features persistent reasoning traces across multi-turn conversations. [Decrypt](https://decrypt.co/364948/alibaba-qwen-3-6-max-preview-most-powerful-model)

### Google Deep Research & Deep Research Max (April 21–22)
Google launched autonomous research agents powered by Gemini 3.1 Pro. Deep Research is optimized for speed in interactive use; Deep Research Max uses extended compute for comprehensive background reports. Both natively generate charts and infographics and support MCP integration. Gemini 3.1 Pro itself scored 77.1% on ARC-AGI-2 and 85.9 on BrowseComp. [SiliconANGLE](https://siliconangle.com/2026/04/22/google-launches-ai-research-agents-powered-gemini-3-1-pro/) · [Google Blog](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/models-and-research/gemini-models/next-generation-gemini-deep-research/)

### Claude Opus 4.7 (April 16) — One Week In
Anthropic's new default Opus model continues its rollout. Key upgrades: high-resolution image support (2576px / 3.75MP, up from 1568px), 13% coding improvement over Opus 4.6, new "task budgets" for agentic workflows, and 1M token context. Pricing unchanged at $5/$25 per million tokens. [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-opus-4-7) · [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/16/anthropic-claude-opus-4-7-model-mythos.html)

### DeepSeek V4 — Still Pending
The ~1T parameter MoE model (~37B active) with a 1M-token context window and multimodal generation has been delayed three times but is now expected in late April. Notably, DeepSeek gave Huawei's Ascend 950PR chips exclusive early access while denying NVIDIA — a deliberate signal amid U.S. export controls. [FindSkill.ai](https://findskill.ai/blog/deepseek-v4-release-date-specs/)

### Claude Mythos Update
Anthropic's Mythos Preview, revealed April 7, remains restricted. It is not publicly available and triggers Anthropic's ASL-4 safety protocol. It's being deployed through Project Glasswing to select cybersecurity partners, where it has identified thousands of zero-day vulnerabilities across major operating systems and browsers. The NSA is using Mythos despite the Pentagon's "supply chain risk" blacklist of Anthropic. Mythos is available on [AWS Bedrock](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/bedrock/latest/userguide/model-card-anthropic-claude-mythos-preview.html) and [Google Vertex AI](https://cloud.google.com/blog/products/ai-machine-learning/claude-mythos-preview-on-vertex-ai) for approved partners. [Foreign Policy](https://foreignpolicy.com/2026/04/20/claude-mythos-preview-anthropic-project-glasswing-cybersecurity-ai-hacking-danger/) · [Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/04/19/nsa-anthropic-mythos-pentagon)

## Key Opinions & Statements

### Dario Amodei — White House Meeting on Mythos
Anthropic's CEO met White House Chief of Staff Susie Wiles and Treasury Secretary Scott Bessent on April 17 for "productive and constructive" talks about Mythos and the ongoing government standoff. Separately, Amodei warned that AI could eliminate half of entry-level white-collar jobs within five years, potentially pushing U.S. unemployment to 10–20%. [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/17/anthropic-dario-amodei-trump-mythos.html) · [Atlanta Daily World](https://atlantadailyworld.com/2026/04/20/anthropic-ceo-warns-that-ai-could-displace-entry-level-roles-within-five-years/)

### Yann LeCun — Rejects Amodei's Jobs Prediction, Downplays Mythos
LeCun called Amodei's 50% job loss prediction "destructive and dangerous," arguing that AI lab CEOs are not the right experts on economic impact. He also dismissed concerns about Claude Mythos as exaggerated. LeCun's startup AMI Labs ($1.03B seed, $3.5B valuation) continues work on world models as an alternative to LLMs. [Startup News](https://startupnews.fyi/2026/04/19/ai-pioneer-yann-lecun-rejects-anthropics-50-percent-job-loss-prediction-destructive-and-dangerous/) · [Storyboard18](https://www.storyboard18.com/brand-makers/meta-ai-chief-yann-lecun-downplays-fears-over-anthropics-claude-mythos-94932.htm)

### Andrej Karpathy — From Code Generation to Knowledge Management
Karpathy has shifted focus from using AI to write code toward building "LLM Wikis" — systems where LLMs maintain interlinked knowledge bases from raw research materials. He also introduced "idea files," structured markdown specs that let AI agents build systems from architectural descriptions rather than code templates. [Medium](https://medium.com/neuralnotions/andrej-karpathy-stopped-using-ai-to-write-code-hes-using-it-to-build-a-second-brain-instead-cddceadc5df5)

### Sam Altman — AI and Hollywood
At the Breakthrough Prize Ceremony, Altman said people will "care more about human creators, not less" as AI advances. He also shut down OpenAI's Sora video platform before Disney could launch characters on it. Altman continues to face personal security threats — a suspect was charged for a firebomb attack on his home on April 10. [Variety](https://variety.com/2026/digital/columns/openai-ceo-sam-altman-ai-in-hollywood-will-get-people-to-care-more-about-human-creators-not-less-1236726413/) · [Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/04/16/anti-ai-sentiment-is-rising-and-its-starting-to-turn-violent/)

### Demis Hassabis — DeepMind's Startup Pace
In his new biography "The Infinity Machine" and on the 20VC podcast, Hassabis described returning DeepMind to startup-speed after the Google Brain merger and maintains a 50% probability estimate for AGI by 2030. He runs a "second workday" starting at 10pm for Isomorphic Labs' drug discovery work. [Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/04/16/demis-hassabis-google-deepmind-chatbot-wars-ai-safety/) · [The Next Web](https://thenextweb.com/news/google-deepmind-hassabis-startup-pace)

# AI Daily Digest — May 6, 2026

**Executive Summary:** Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei warned of a "moment of danger" as Claude Mythos has uncovered tens of thousands of software vulnerabilities, giving institutions a 6–12 month window to patch before Chinese AI models catch up. Meanwhile, OpenAI launched GPT-5.5 Instant and Google DeepMind UK workers voted to unionize over military AI contracts.

## Notable Papers & Research

**DeepSeek V4 Pro** — DeepSeek AI
Open-weights MoE model (1.6T params, 49B activated) achieving SWE-bench 80.6% and Codeforces 3206, edging Claude Opus 4.7 in coding at 10x lower cost. NIST's CAISI evaluation places it roughly on par with GPT-5 overall, about 8 months behind the US frontier.
[NIST Evaluation](https://www.nist.gov/news-events/news/2026/05/caisi-evaluation-deepseek-v4-pro) | [HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/deepseek-ai/DeepSeek-V4-Pro)

**Thinking with Visual Primitives** — DeepSeek AI
Framework integrating points and bounding boxes as fundamental reasoning units in multimodal LLMs, addressing the "Reference Gap" in complex visual reasoning tasks like counting, spatial deduction, and navigation.
[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/current)

**Agentic Harness Engineering (AHE)**
Closed-loop system for automatically evolving external components of coding agents (prompts, tools, middleware), improving pass@1 on Terminal-Bench 2 from 69.7% to 77.0%.
[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/current)

## Key Opinions & Statements

### Dario Amodei (Anthropic CEO)
**"A moment of danger"** — Warned institutions have a 6–12 month window to fix thousands of vulnerabilities found by Mythos before Chinese AI models develop similar capabilities. Said the risk is "some enormous increase in the amount of vulnerabilities, in the amount of breaches, in the financial damage that's done from ransomware on schools, hospitals, not to mention banks."

Also invoked the Jevons Paradox on employment, shifting from his earlier warnings about white-collar job elimination: "If you automate 90% of the job, then everyone does the 10% of the job."
[CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/05/anthropic-ceo-cyber-moment-of-danger-mythos-vulnerabilities.html) | [Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/05/05/dario-amodei-jevons-paradox-will-ai-wipe-out-white-collar-jobs/)

### Yann LeCun (Meta, outgoing Chief AI Scientist)
Called the prediction that AI will erase 20% of jobs "ridiculously stupid" and warned that doom narratives from AI CEOs are "extremely destructive" — pushing teens into depression and bad career decisions. Specifically rebutted Amodei's entry-level job loss claims. Advised students to still attend college and study physics or electrical engineering. Said AI is "still not very good at reasoning" and human-level AI is "not going to happen for quite a while."
[Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/05/05/ai-godfather-says-ceos-hyping-job-loss-are-extremely-destructive-and-your-kids-are-paying-the-price) | [Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/05/04/ai-godfather-survival-guide-hype-doom)

### Andrej Karpathy
At Sequoia AI Ascent 2026, described December 2025 as the inflection point for agentic coding: "I can't remember the last time I corrected the model." Argues that as AI improves, human understanding becomes the bottleneck — developers still need depth to "know what to ask, what to inspect, what to reject."
[Karpathy Blog](https://karpathy.bearblog.dev/sequoia-ascent-2026/) | [Substack](https://aiagentssimplified.substack.com/p/from-vibe-coding-to-agentic-engineering)

### Sam Altman (OpenAI CEO)
Warned about "AI washing" — companies falsely attributing layoffs to AI when the technology isn't actually responsible. This contrasts with simultaneous GPT-5.5 Instant launch marketing emphasizing productivity gains.
[Fortune](https://fortune.com/article/sam-altman-ai-washing-tech-layoffs/)

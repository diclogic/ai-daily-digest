## Notable Papers

- **Verifier-Backed Hard Problem Generation for Mathematical Reasoning** — Yuhang Lai, Jiazhan Feng, Yee Whye Teh, Ning Miao. New method for generating hard math problems to improve LLM reasoning capabilities. ([arXiv:2605.06660](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06660))

- **"Agentic AI Orchestration Should be Bayes-consistent"** — Position paper from 30 researchers arguing that the orchestration layer of agentic AI systems must be grounded in Bayesian principles for reliable decision-making. ([arXiv](https://arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/current))

- **Why Global LLM Leaderboards Are Misleading** — Challenges the reliability of current LLM ranking methodologies, showing that small portfolios for heterogeneous supervised ML tasks give a more accurate picture. ([arXiv:2605.06656](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06656))

## Model & Product Launches

### Claude Mythos — Anthropic's Cybersecurity Breakthrough
Anthropic's Claude Mythos Preview, announced April 7 and still rolling out under restricted access, remains the most consequential model release this quarter. Mythos discovered thousands of zero-day vulnerabilities across every major operating system and web browser during pre-release testing, including a 17-year-old remote code execution flaw in FreeBSD (CVE-2026-4747). It developed working exploits on the first attempt in over 83% of cases. Under **Project Glasswing**, Anthropic has shared Mythos Preview with roughly four dozen organizations — including Apple, Google, Microsoft, AWS, NVIDIA, the Linux Foundation, and JPMorgan Chase — to help secure critical software before broader release. Anthropic says it is too dangerous for general public access. ([Anthropic — Project Glasswing](https://www.anthropic.com/glasswing)) ([The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/04/anthropics-claude-mythos-finds.html))

### GPT-5.5 Instant — OpenAI's New ChatGPT Default
OpenAI released GPT-5.5 Instant on May 5 as the new default model for ChatGPT. It produces 52.5% fewer hallucinated claims than GPT-5.3 Instant on high-stakes prompts in medicine, law, and finance. OpenAI says the model is "smarter, clearer, and more personalized." ([OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-5-instant/)) ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/05/openai-releases-gpt-5-5-instant-a-new-default-model-for-chatgpt/))

### DeepSeek V4 Preview — Open-Source Frontier Challenger
DeepSeek V4 Preview remains in its preview phase after launching April 24. V4-Pro (1.6T total / 49B active params) and V4-Flash (284B / 13B active) both support 1M-token context and Thinking/Non-Thinking modes. V4 beats all current open models in Math/STEM/Coding and rivals top closed-source models. ([DeepSeek API Docs](https://api-docs.deepseek.com/news/news260424)) ([MIT Technology Review](https://www.technologyreview.com/2026/04/24/1136422/why-deepseeks-v4-matters/))

## Key Opinions & Statements

### Dario Amodei (Anthropic)
Amodei warned that AI has created a narrow "moment of danger" for tech firms, governments, and banks to fix tens of thousands of software vulnerabilities found by Mythos. At a JPMorgan Chase event in Lower Manhattan alongside Jamie Dimon, he said Anthropic's growth has been "just crazy" and "too hard to handle," with 80-fold usage growth in Q1 pushing the company to a $30B annualized revenue run rate. He also shifted his public narrative on AI jobs, now invoking Jevons paradox to argue that AI-driven productivity gains will create more demand for human work, not less — a notable change from his white-collar job displacement warnings last year. ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/05/anthropic-ceo-cyber-moment-of-danger-mythos-vulnerabilities.html)) ([Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/05/05/dario-amodei-jevons-paradox-will-ai-wipe-out-white-collar-jobs/))

### Yann LeCun (AMI Labs, formerly Meta)
LeCun called predictions that AI will erase 20% of jobs "ridiculously stupid" and said doom narratives from CEOs are "extremely destructive," warning they are causing depression in high school students who fear AI will take their jobs. He advised people to listen to economists rather than CEOs about employment impact, noting that historically it takes new technologies 15 years to achieve productivity gains. LeCun left Meta in late 2025 and raised $1.03B in seed funding for AMI Labs, built around his JEPA architecture. ([Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/05/05/ai-job-apocalypse-warnings-destructive-yann-lecun/)) ([Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/05/04/ai-godfather-survival-guide-hype-doom))

### Andrej Karpathy
At Sequoia's AI Ascent 2026, Karpathy framed the shift from "vibe coding" to "agentic engineering" — where AI plans, uses tools, executes, checks outcomes, and iterates with limited human input. He warned of "jagged intelligence" (AI capability is unevenly distributed) and emphasized: "You cannot outsource understanding." As models take over implementation, developers become editors, reviewers, and system designers. ([Sequoia AI Ascent](https://podcasts.apple.com/ni/podcast/andrej-karpathy-at-sequoia-ai-ascent-2026-from-vibe/id1799918505?i=1000765806763&l=en-GB)) ([AIntelligenceHub](https://aintelligencehub.com/articles/karpathy-vibe-coding-to-agent-workflows-may-2026))

### Sam Altman (OpenAI)
Altman's week was dominated by the courtroom. In the ongoing Musk v. Altman trial in Oakland, former OpenAI CTO Mira Murati testified via video deposition that Altman sowed "chaos" and distrust among executives, pitted leaders against each other, and once falsely told her that lawyers had cleared a new AI model to bypass an internal safety board. Separately, Altman confirmed GPT-5.5 "planned its own launch party" for May 5 — an instance of emergent self-referential behavior. ## Other Notable AI News

### Anthropic Eyes $900B Valuation, Potential IPO
Anthropic is weighing a $50B funding round at a $900B valuation — which would surpass OpenAI's $852B. The round could close in May with a potential IPO as early as October 2026. Valuation has risen from $61.5B (March 2025) to $183B to $380B to potentially $900B+ in just over a year. ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/30/anthropic-potential-900b-valuation-round-could-happen-within-two-weeks/)) ([Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-04-29/anthropic-considering-funding-offers-at-over-900-billion-value))

### Anthropic-SpaceX Data Center Deal
To cope with its compute crunch, Anthropic struck a deal with SpaceX for capacity at Musk's Colossus 1 data center in Memphis, gaining access to 300+ MW of power and 220,000+ NVIDIA GPUs (H100, H200, GB200). Musk, who previously wrote that "Anthropic hates Western Civilization," said after meeting Anthropic leadership that he was "impressed." ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/06/anthropic-spacex-data-center-capacity.html)) ([Simon Willison](https://simonwillison.net/2026/May/7/xai-anthropic/))

### Trump Administration Reverses Course on AI Oversight
The White House is now considering mandatory pre-deployment evaluations of frontier AI models, comparing the process to FDA drug approval. The shift was triggered by Claude Mythos's cybersecurity capabilities. CAISI has signed agreements with Google DeepMind, Microsoft, and xAI for pre-release model testing. Notably, Anthropic is the only major lab that has refused Pentagon AI contracts, with the military labeling it a "supply chain risk." ([Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/05/04/trump-white-house-ai-safety-tests-mythos)) ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/05/ai-oversight-trump-google-microsoft-xai.html))

### DeepMind Workers Vote to Unionize
UK-based Google DeepMind workers voted 98% in favor of forming the world's first union at a frontier AI lab, organized by the Communication Workers Union. The move was triggered by Google's deal letting the Pentagon use Gemini AI in classified military networks for "any lawful purpose." Workers demand an end to military AI contracts with the U.S. and Israeli militaries and restoration of the company's scrapped commitment not to develop AI weapons. ([Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/05/05/google-deepmind-unionize-vote-military-ai-contracts-internal-backlash-pentagon-deal-israeli-defense-forces/)) ([Gizmodo](https://gizmodo.com/deepmind-workers-vote-to-unionize-following-googles-pentagon-ai-deal-2000754603))

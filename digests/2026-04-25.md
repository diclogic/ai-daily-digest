## Notable Papers

- **The AI Scientist-v2: Workshop-Level Automated Scientific Discovery via Agentic Tree Search** — Yamada, Lange et al. (Sakana AI). An end-to-end agentic system that autonomously formulates hypotheses, runs experiments, and writes papers. One fully AI-generated manuscript was accepted at an ICLR workshop — the first such peer-reviewed acceptance. [arXiv 2504.08066](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.08066)

- **AltTrain: Safety Alignment for Large Reasoning Models** — Proposes altering the reasoning structure of LRMs via supervised fine-tuning with just 1K examples, avoiding complex RL. Shows that harmful outputs from reasoning models stem from the reasoning structure itself, not the final answer layer. [arXiv (April 2026)](https://arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/current)

- **Forecast Collapse of Transformer-Based Models Under Squared Loss** — Andreoletti (2026). A formal proof that Transformer architectures exhibit forecast collapse under squared loss, with implications for financial and time-series forecasting applications. [arXiv (April 2026)](https://arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/current)

- **MedGemma 1.5 Technical Report** — Google's updated medical foundation model for clinical AI applications. [Hugging Face Papers](https://huggingface.co/papers/trending)

## Model & Product Launches

### OpenAI GPT-5.5 (April 23)
OpenAI released GPT-5.5 and GPT-5.5 Pro, its latest flagship models with improved coding, computer use, and deep research capabilities. Rolling out to Plus, Pro, Business, and Enterprise subscribers in ChatGPT and Codex, with API access available. [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/23/openai-announces-latest-artificial-intelligence-model.html) | [OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-5-5/)

### DeepSeek V4 (April 24)
DeepSeek released V4-Pro (1.6T total params, 49B active) and V4-Flash (284B total, 13B active), both with 1M token context. V4-Pro scores 80.6% on SWE-bench Verified — within 0.2 points of Claude Opus 4.6. Pricing is aggressive: $3.48/M output tokens (Pro) and $0.28/M (Flash). Open-source and optimized for Huawei's domestic chips. [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/24/deepseek-v4-llm-preview-open-source-ai-competition-china.html) | [Simon Willison](https://simonwillison.net/2026/Apr/24/deepseek-v4/)

### Claude Mythos Update
No new public release. Claude Mythos Preview remains restricted to ~40 organizations via Project Glasswing for defensive cybersecurity. An unauthorized breach via a third-party vendor environment is under investigation. The model has identified tens of thousands of zero-day vulnerabilities across every major OS and browser. No public launch date committed. [Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/04/23/anthropic-mythos-leak-dario-amodei-ceo-cybersecurity-hackers-exploits-ai/) | [Foreign Policy](https://foreignpolicy.com/2026/04/20/claude-mythos-preview-anthropic-project-glasswing-cybersecurity-ai-hacking-danger/)

## Key Opinions & Statements

### Sam Altman
Altman apologized to the Tumbler Ridge, BC community for OpenAI's failure to alert law enforcement about a ChatGPT account banned in June 2025 — eight months before the user carried out a mass shooting at a school in February 2026 that killed eight people. "I am deeply sorry that we did not alert law enforcement," Altman said. Separately, the Musk v. Altman trial begins next week in Oakland. [CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2026/04/24/world/sam-altman-openai-apologize-tumbler-ridge) | [SF Standard](https://sfstandard.com/2026/04/24/elon-openai-trial/)

### Dario Amodei
Amodei told Axios that AI could eliminate half of all entry-level white-collar jobs within five years, potentially pushing U.S. unemployment to 10-20%. "We, as the producers of this technology, have a duty and an obligation to be honest about what is coming." He also met with White House officials on April 17 to discuss Claude Mythos and safety. [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/17/anthropic-dario-amodei-trump-mythos.html) | [TheStreet](https://www.thestreet.com/technology/anthropic-ceo-makes-shocking-admission-about-ai)

### Yann LeCun
LeCun dismissed concerns around Claude Mythos as "exaggerated and unnecessary drama." He continues to argue that LLMs are not the path to human-level intelligence, advocating instead for Joint Embedding Predictive Architecture (JEPA) and world models through his new startup AMI Labs ($1.03B seed round, $3.5B valuation). [Storyboard18](https://www.storyboard18.com/brand-makers/meta-ai-chief-yann-lecun-downplays-fears-over-anthropics-claude-mythos-94932.htm) | [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/09/yann-lecuns-ami-labs-raises-1-03-billion-to-build-world-models/)

### Demis Hassabis
Hassabis described Google DeepMind's acceleration over the past 2-3 years on the 20VC podcast, crediting the Brain merger for combining compute resources with DeepMind's research culture. He will visit Korea on April 29 — his first return since the 2016 AlphaGo match — to speak at Google for Korea 2026. [Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/04/16/demis-hassabis-google-deepmind-chatbot-wars-ai-safety/) | [Seoul Economic Daily](https://en.sedaily.com/technology/2026/04/24/alphago-father-hassabis-returns-to-korea-after-10-years-to)

### Andrej Karpathy
Karpathy shared that he's shifted from using AI to generate code to using it to organize knowledge — building interlinked wikis from raw research materials. His research wiki has grown to ~100 articles and 400K words, maintained almost entirely by an LLM agent. He published the full architecture as a GitHub gist. [Analytics Vidhya](https://www.analyticsvidhya.com/blog/2026/04/llm-wiki-by-andrej-karpathy/)

### Ilya Sutskever

Altman trial beginning next week. [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2025/04/12/openai-co-founder-ilya-sutskevers-safe-superintelligence-reportedly-valued-at-32b/)
